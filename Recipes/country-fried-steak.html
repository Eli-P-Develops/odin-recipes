Pepperoni Pizza

Background of Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni pizza is an Italian-inspired dish popularized in the United States. Pepperoni sausage was originally created to emulate Italian sausage and salami but quickly became popularized as a topping for pizza in upstate New York. Today, pepperoni is the #1 most popular pizza topping of choice and a universally known classic in American cuisine.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 18 to 20 slices pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese, grated
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon fresh oregano, optional
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500Â°F for 20 minutes
  2. Roll out dough on a lightly floured surface
  3. Add layer of sauce onto pizza dough along with pepperoni. Top with grated cheese and more pepperoni. To finish off toppings, add black pepper
  4. Place pizza into preheated oven for 6-8 minutes. Check it periodically
  5. Slide pizza out onto a cutting board and cut into slices. Let rest for a 1-2 minutes to cool
  6. Add fresh oregano if needed
  7. Enjoy!
