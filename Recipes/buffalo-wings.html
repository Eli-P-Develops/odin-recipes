Buffalo Wings

Background of Buffalo Wings

Buffalo wings were first created in 1964 at a bar in Buffalo, New York called the Anchor Bar. Buffalo wings are deep fried chicken wings covered in a tangy, sometimes spicy, sauce. This signature entree/snack food has since become a popular menu item in American bars and restruants across the nation.

Ingredients

    Wings

  • 4lb chicken wings
  • 5 teaspoons baking powder
  • 3/4 teaspoons salt

    • Sauce

  • 1/2 cup Franks Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoons salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250°F for 10-15 minutes
  2. Line baking sheet with aluminum foil and spray with vegetable oil
  3. Place wings into large bowl. Sprinkle baking powder and salt. Toss until fully covered
  4. Place wings into lower rack of the oven for 30 minutes. Remove from lower rack, turn over heat to 425°F, and place wings on top shelf. Bake for another 40 to 50 minutes
  5. Remove wings when golden brown and crispy
  6. Stir together sauce ingredients and cover wings
  7. Enjoy!
